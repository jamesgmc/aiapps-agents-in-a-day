How to Play

  1. Register: Enter your name and click Register, or reconnect with your Player ID
  2. Wait: Wait for the tournament to start (referee controls this)
  3. Manual Play: When a round starts, you'll see a confirmation popup
  4. Answer Questions: Review and modify the suggested answer if needed
  5. Choose Move: Select Rock, Paper, or Scissors for your move
  6. Submit: Confirm your submission or cancel to wait
  7. Results: View your results after each round
  8. Final Results: See your final ranking with medals, podium, and complete leaderboard when tournament ends!

Note: Make sure the game server is running on http://localhost:5289

Multi-Window: You can open multiple browser windows to play as different players
